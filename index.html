Discover the beauty, culture, and adventure awaiting in Gangtok & Darjeeling. This guide provides all the information you need to plan a perfect trip filled with memories. Enjoy handpicked places to visit, insider tips, and personalized itineraries.

Explore Gangtok

Gangtok, the capital of Sikkim, is a blend of traditional charm and modern cityscape. With majestic mountains and serene monasteries, it offers something for every traveler.

MG Road

The central hub of Gangtok, full of shopping, local eateries, and a relaxed vibe. Perfect for an evening stroll.

Tsomgo Lake

A high-altitude lake surrounded by mountains. You can enjoy yak rides and snow in winter.

Rumtek Monastery

Spiritual and architecturally stunning. A peaceful retreat into Sikkim's Buddhist culture.

Hanuman Tok

A temple offering one of the best panoramic views of Gangtok and the surrounding hills.

Namgyal Institute of Tibetology

A research institute and museum that provides insights into Tibetan culture, history, and art.

Banjhakri Falls

A beautiful landscaped park with waterfalls, perfect for a relaxing afternoon walk.

Discover Darjeeling

Known as the "Queen of Hills," Darjeeling is a picturesque hill station famous for its tea, Himalayan views, and colonial charm.

Tiger Hill

Famous for its sunrise views over Mt. Kanchenjunga. A must-visit for photographers and nature lovers.

Himalayan Railway

A UNESCO World Heritage site offering toy train rides through hills and tea gardens.

Peace Pagoda

A serene Japanese Buddhist temple showcasing beautiful sculptures and peaceful surroundings.

Darjeeling Zoo

Home to rare Himalayan species like snow leopards and red pandas. Great for a family day out.

Tea Estates

Visit tea gardens like Happy Valley for guided tours and fresh Darjeeling tea tasting experiences.

Rock Garden & Ganga Maya Park

A terraced garden with waterfalls and a lovely picnic spot just a short drive from Darjeeling.
